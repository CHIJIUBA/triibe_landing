Task: Create a Landing Page
We would like you to create a landing page for a fictional product or service. This task will help us evaluate your proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, 
as well as your design sensibility.

Task Details:
Product/Service: [Example: "Eco-friendly Reusable Water Bottles"]
Design: The page should be visually appealing, responsive, and user-friendly. Feel free to use your creativity, but ensure that it aligns with modern design principles.

Content Sections: The landing page should include the following sections:
Header: Logo, navigation menu, and a call-to-action button.

Hero Section: A large image or video, a headline, and a subheading that highlights the main value proposition.
Features Section: A brief overview of the product/service features with icons or images.
Testimonials: A section with 2-3 customer testimonials.

Call to Action: A final section encouraging users to take action (e.g., sign up, purchase, etc.).
Footer: Contact information, social media links, and additional navigation links.



Technologies: You can use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. You are welcome to use any libraries or frameworks you are familiar with, but please document them.
Deadline: Please submit your completed task by 19th, Friday, August 2024.
Submission: Send us the files in a .zip format or upload them to a repository (e.g., GitHub) and share the link with us.
We are not only looking at the final product but also how you approach the task, your attention to detail, and your ability to deliver a clean, functional design.
